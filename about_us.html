Faculty

St. Xavier’s School, can boast of a qualified and experienced team of teachers striving for the overall development of their pupils. They live with the children and ensure the atmosphere of love and care. Faith in the Fatherhood of God and Brotherhood of man, is instilled through Human Value classes, prayers, stories and parables drawn from different religions of the world and through the very atmosphere of the school. Value education and philosophy for children programme of Montclair State University, USA are integrated with the entire school programme. Our Schools are rightly proud of a good academic record over the years. Our class X students achieved 100% result during past three years under CBSE.
